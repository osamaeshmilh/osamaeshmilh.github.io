Angry Port Scanner Support

Welcome to the support page for Angry Port Scanner.

About the App

Angry Port Scanner helps you scan your local Wi-Fi network, discover connected devices, and check open ports directly from your device.

How to Use

  1. Connect your iPhone or iPad to a Wi-Fi network.
  2. Open Angry Port Scanner.
  3. Allow Local Network permission when iOS asks.
  4. Confirm or edit the IP range.
  5. Tap Scan to discover devices and open ports.

Local Network Permission

The app needs Local Network permission to discover devices on your own Wi-Fi network. All scans run locally on your device. No scan data is uploaded or shared.

Common Issues

No devices found

Make sure your device is connected to Wi-Fi and Local Network permission is enabled in iOS Settings.

Some devices are missing

Some devices block network requests or do not advertise services. Try increasing the scan timeout or scanning a smaller IP range.

Open ports are not showing

The device may have no open ports in the selected range, or a firewall may be blocking the scan.
