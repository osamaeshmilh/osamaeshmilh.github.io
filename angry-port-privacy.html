Privacy Policy

App: Angry Port Scanner

Last Updated: June 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Angry Port Scanner is a local network and port scanning app designed to help users discover devices and open ports on their own Wi-Fi network.

2. Data Collection

Angry Port Scanner does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

The app does not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Usage analytics
  • Device identifiers
  • IP scan results
  • Network history

3. Local Network Access

The app requests Local Network permission on iOS to scan the user's own Wi-Fi network, discover nearby devices, and detect open ports.

All scanning is performed locally on the user's device. No scan results are uploaded or sent to any server.

4. Scan History

If scan history is saved, it is stored only on the user's device. The user can clear scan history at any time from inside the app.

5. No Accounts

Angry Port Scanner does not require users to create an account or sign in.

6. No Ads or Tracking

The app does not display ads, use analytics, or include third-party tracking SDKs.

7. Bonjour / mDNS

The app may use Bonjour / mDNS discovery to identify devices and services available on the local network. This information is processed only on the device.

8. Third-Party Services

Angry Port Scanner does not share data with third-party services.

9. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
