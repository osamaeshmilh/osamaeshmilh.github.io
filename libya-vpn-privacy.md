# Privacy Policy for LibyaVPN

**Last Updated:** December 16, 2025

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to LibyaVPN. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a secure, private, and unrestricted internet experience. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

## 2. No-Logs Policy
**We adhere to a strict No-Logs Policy.**
We do **NOT** collect, store, or share any of the following:
*   YOUR IP address.
*   The websites you visit.
*   The data you transfer (files, emails, messages).
*   DNS queries.
*   Connection timestamps or session durations.

## 3. Data We Do Collect (Minimal)
To provide our service, we only collect the absolute minimum data required for technical functioning:
*   **Account Information:** If you create an account, we may store your username/email solely for authentication purposes.
*   **Active Connection Status:** We may temporarily monitor whether a user is currently "active" solely to enforce concurrent connection limits (if applicable). This data is wiped instantly when you disconnect.

## 4. How We Use Your Data
Since we collect minimal data, our usage is strictly limited to:
*   Authenticating your user account.
*   Troubleshooting technical issues (only upon your specific request).
*   Improving network stability (using aggregated, non-identifiable data).

## 5. Third-Party Sharing
We do **NOT** sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. We do not use third-party analytics or advertising trackers in our app.

## 6. Security
We use industry-standard **WireGuard®** protocol with state-of-the-art cryptography (ChaCha20, Poly1305, Curve25519) to encrypt your connection. Your data is secure from ISPs, hackers, and surveillance.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy strictly to reflect changes in our service or legal requirements. You will be notified of any significant changes.
